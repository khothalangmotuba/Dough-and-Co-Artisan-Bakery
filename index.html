My Story

How I Went from Baking in My Kitchen to Opening My Own Bakery

Hey, I'm Khothalang Motuba, and I've always been obsessed with bread.

My grandmother is still alive and she's the one who taught me everything I know. Every Saturday morning, I would go to her house and watch her work the dough. The whole house would fill with this warm, yeasty smell that felt like home. She never used a recipe—just instinct and years of experience. I sat on her kitchen counter watching, learning, and falling in love with the craft.

In 2024, I decided to turn my passion into something real. My grandmother gave me her blessing and said, "Go make something of yourself, my child." So I started baking from my home kitchen in Leribe Hlotse Lisement, and before I knew it, neighbors were knocking on my door asking for more.

What began as a way to honor my grandmother's teachings has grown into something I never expected: a real business.

What I Stand For

Quality First

No preservatives, no additives, no shortcuts. 24-hour minimum fermentation. Hand-shaped, never machine-made.

Local Always

Every ingredient sourced locally. Supporting farmers in our community. Seasonal recipes that change with harvest.

Community Focused

Free bread for local shelters. Baking workshops for students. Giving back to Leribe Hlotse.
